## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Safety Concerns
- **Mitigation:** Strict content filters, human review, parental controls, COPPA compliance

### Risk 2: Competition
- **Mitigation:** Focus on safety, quality, brand loyalty, community building

### Risk 3: User Retention
- **Mitigation:** Gamification, personalization, regular content updates, community engagement

### Risk 4: Technical Scaling
- **Mitigation:** Start with scalable architecture, load testing, gradual growth

### Risk 5: Monetization
- **Mitigation:** Freemium model, clear value proposition, free tier for discovery
