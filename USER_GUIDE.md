# DrawSpark - User Guide

## Welcome to DrawSpark! 🎨

DrawSpark is an AI-powered drawing prompt generator designed to inspire creativity in kids (ages 4-14). Our prompts are safe, age-appropriate, and designed to spark imagination without being overwhelming.

---

## Getting Started

### How to Use DrawSpark

1. **Visit DrawSpark**: Go to the DrawSpark website (or deploy locally)
2. **Select Age**: Choose the child's age (4-14)
3. **Choose Category**: Pick a drawing category (Animals, Fantasy, Sports, etc.)
4. **Select Difficulty**: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard
5. **Click "Generate Prompt"**: Get your creative prompt!
6. **Start Drawing!** Let creativity flow

## Difficulty Levels

### Easy (Ages 4-6)
- Simple prompts
- Basic shapes
- Familiar subjects
- 1-2 elements in each prompt

**Example:** "Draw a cute cat playing with a ball of yarn."

### Medium (Ages 7-10)
- More detail
- Creative combinations
- Action scenes
- 2-3 elements in each prompt

**Example:** "Draw a friendly dragon flying over a castle while chasing a butterfly."

### Hard (Ages 11-14)
- Complex scenes
- Multiple elements
- Creative challenges
- Perspective and composition
- 3-4 elements in each prompt

**Example:** "Draw a futuristic city with flying cars, robot delivery people, and a giant observation tower shaped like a rocket."

## Tips for Parents

### Encourage, Don't Correct
- Focus on effort and creativity
- Praise the process, not just the result
- Let them explore and make mistakes

### Provide Variety
- Rotate through different categories
- Try different difficulty levels
- Mix solo drawing with family drawing time

### Extend the Learning
- Ask questions about their drawing
- Have them create a story about what they drew
- Use the prompt as a writing prompt after drawing

### Display Their Art
- Create a "wall of fame" at home
- Take photos to share with family
- Keep a portfolio of their best work

### Make It a Routine
- Daily drawing time (15-30 minutes)
- Weekend drawing challenges
- Special drawing sessions for holidays

## Tips for Kids

### Start Simple
- Use basic shapes (circles, squares, triangles)
- Draw light lines first, then darken the ones you like
- Don't worry about making it perfect

### Use Your Imagination
- Add your own details to the prompt
- Change colors to what you like
- Combine ideas from different prompts

### Practice Makes Perfect
- Draw the same prompt multiple times
- Try different styles (cartoon, realistic)
- Learn from your mistakes

### Have Fun!
- There's no wrong way to draw
- Your art is unique to you
- The most important thing is enjoying yourself

## Troubleshooting

### Prompt Doesn't Inspire
- Try a different category
- Adjust the difficulty level
- Click "Generate Prompt" again for a new idea

### Child Can't Decide What to Draw
- Let them choose the category first
- Offer 2-3 options and let them pick
- Use the "random" option if available

### Drawing Seems Too Hard
- Break it down into simple shapes
- Draw just one part of the prompt
- Try a lower difficulty level

## FAQ

**Q: What age is DrawSpark for?**
A: DrawSpark is designed for kids ages 4-14, with prompts tailored to each age group.

**Q: Do I need to be good at drawing to use DrawSpark?**
A: No! DrawSpark is for all skill levels. The prompts are designed to inspire creativity, not test artistic ability.

**Q: Can adults use DrawSpark?**
A: Absolutely! Adults enjoy DrawSpark too - it's great for stress relief, creative breaks, and family drawing time.

**Q: How often can I generate prompts?**
A: Free tier: 10 prompts per day. Premium: Unlimited prompts.

**Q: Can I share my artwork?**
A: Yes! We encourage sharing artwork with family and friends. We also have a community gallery for premium users.

**Q: What if I don't like the prompt I got?**
A: No problem! Just click "Generate Prompt" again to get a new idea. There's no limit to trying different prompts.

**Q: Is DrawSpark educational?**
A: Yes! Drawing helps develop fine motor skills, creativity, self-expression, and can even improve focus and concentration.
