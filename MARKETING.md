# DrawSpark - Marketing Strategy

## Product Positioning

**DrawSpark** is an AI-powered drawing prompt generator designed specifically for kids (ages 4-14). It provides safe, age-appropriate, and creative drawing prompts that inspire creativity and artistic expression.

### Value Proposition
- **Safe Content**: All prompts are pre-vetted and age-appropriate
- **Age-Adaptive**: Prompts adjust difficulty based on the child's age
- **Creative Freedom**: Inspires imagination without overwhelming detail
- **Educational**: Encourages artistic development and storytelling
- **Fun**: Kids love the variety and surprise of new prompts

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Kids (4-14)
- Love drawing and creativity
- Need inspiration for what to draw
- Want variety in their art
- Enjoy the "surprise" element

### Secondary: Parents
- Want screen-free activities
- Need ideas to keep kids occupied
- Value educational content
- Looking for creative tools

### Tertiary: Teachers & Schools
- Art class inspiration
- Brain break activities
- Creative writing prompts (draw first, then write story)
- Homeschooling resources

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Twitter/X (Primary)
**Strategy:** Educational content + product showcases

**Content Types:**
- Daily drawing prompts with example outputs
- Kid artwork showcase (with parent permission)
- Art education tips
- Product updates and features
- Behind-the-scenes development

**Hashtags:** #KidsArt #DrawingPrompts #CreativeKids #ArtForKids #ParentingTips

**Posting Schedule:** 2-3 tweets per day

---

### 2. Instagram (Visual-First)
**Strategy:** Showcase prompts and resulting artwork

**Content Types:**
- Reels: Time-lapse drawings based on prompts
- Carousels: Step-by-step drawing guides
- Stories: Daily prompts with interactive polls
- Posts: Featured artwork and prompt templates

**Hashtags:** #KidsArt #DrawingForKids #CreativeDrawing #ArtPrompts #KidsCreativity

---

### 3. Pinterest (Discovery)

**Content Types:**
- Pin templates for each drawing category

### Week 1: Awareness
